CAROLINE BUSTA

Caroline Busta is a Berlin-based writer, editor, and consultant working with questions of culture, technology, and globalism. She is the founder of New Models, a media outlet for the critical analysis of art, tech, politics, and pop-culture. From 2014 to 2017, she served as the editor-in-chief of the Berlin-based critical art journal Texte zur Kunst, and previously as an associate editor of Artforum magazine in New York.
