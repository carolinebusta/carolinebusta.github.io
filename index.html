CAROLINE BUSTA

Berlin-based writer and editor. Co-founder of New Models, a media channel and community addressing the emergent effects of networked technology on culture. Previously, editor-in-chief of the Berlin-based critical art journal Texte zur Kunst (2014-17) and an associate editor at Artforum magazine (2008-14) in New York.
